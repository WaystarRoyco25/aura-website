Core Functionalities

Engineered for growth and security.

Scenario-Based Social Learning

Access over 100 interactive, multiple-choice modules designed to simulate common social scenarios. This systematic approach allows users to practice and internalize nuanced social cues in a controlled, stress-free environment, reinforcing positive interaction patterns through cognitive repetition.

Geospatial Safety Network

Our integrated mapping system provides real-time location awareness. By interfacing with the Google Maps API, the application can instantly identify and route to the nearest pre-vetted support locations, such as hospitals or community centers, providing a critical layer of security for users and peace of mind for caregivers.
