Our Story & Mission

Our Story

Aura was born from my volunteer work over several years with the nonprofit organization "Learn Together Dream Together," supporting children on the autism spectrum. Through this experience, I saw how important it is for them to have a safe, engaging way to practice social skills for everyday situations.
